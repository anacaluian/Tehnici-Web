Introduction

Animals are found in every part of the world ,from the warm rain forests to the freezing poles ,and from high mountains to the deepest oceans.

This page tells you all about the amazing world of animals.You can read about the many different places where animals live ,and find out how animals survive in their surroundings.You can also learn how many animals are under threat and what we can do to help them.
